Target Audience

The target audience:

  • Businesses and organizations of any size, such as restaurants, schools, libraries, shops, etc. that desire to be closer connected to thier community and have a stronger voice.

  • Citizens and visitors who wish to stay updated with town or business news, help the community, or just have an easy time finding the best locations around the town.

Personas:

Jenny is a mother of three, 35 years old, likes socializing with friends but doesn't have much time for it. She also likes the outdoors and visiting new places.

Jackson is a small shop owner in town, 47 years old, trying to improve his pizza shop and increase sales. Likes working with food and wants to network more with other restaurants nearby.

Scenarios:

  • Why are Chambers of commerce important?

  • What are the goals of a Chamber of commerce?

  • What types of shops and organizations are included in this Chamber of commerce?

  • What are the benefits for businesses and individuals?

  • How can businesses learn about network opportunities using the chamber of commerce?
